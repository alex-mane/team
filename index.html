Our owners were faced with the need to make a lot of crypto transactions and exchange cryptocurrency really quickly. This is how Cryptochange was born.

We have been developing our MVP for almost six months to take into account all the problems that exist in the crypto payments and exchange market.

Pitching at Crypto Expo 2021
We presented our project at Crypto Expo 2021 in Seoul for the first time and received a lot of positive feedback, as well as constructive criticism, which helped us to move forward.

Advisors on Board
Our advisors are John Asper from Bank of America and William Jameson, a renowned financier who has worked for Morgan Stanley and Barclays.

Cryptochange ICO
We released our CRP token that will be used on the Cryptochange platform as means of payment and will be traded on Binance and other trading platforms.

Further Development
After the ICO is over, we'll keep working on our Cryptochange exchange platform, add integrations with multiple wallets, like Guarda, Atomic, Lumi, etc.

Once the development is over, we will fully release the Cryptochange platform and start the bug bounty program. Report a bug and get CRP tokens!
